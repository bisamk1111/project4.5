Truths and Lies

The lowdown on this website

Hi! The website name might be ominous but I think we'll be fine. Since a resume has to be one of the pages in my website, I've decided to make this website about me. Unfortunately, I am quite shy and yes, even though I am in an entirely different country it still applied. Therefore, I have decided to.. hide accurate facts about myself with facts about me I think would be cool one day. Thus the title Truths and Lies.

In the begnning

The begining for me was the 1st of January 2001.

I was born in India, grew up in Singapore and moved to Canada when I was 12. A bit in between that time I lived in new york for a year or so. But, we're finally settled in BC.

An interesting Fact

India does not do daylight savings but Canada does. According to my mum, I was born around 1.30pm. Here's the tricky part, normally Haryana is 12 and a half hours ahead of Vancouver, but in the winter the clocks go back an hour and vancouver is behind 13 and a half hours. So it's totally possible I was born in 2000 and not 2001. Or maybe this is a friend's story and not mine.
